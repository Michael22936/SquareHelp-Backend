What is SquareHelp?

SquareHelp is a tool designed for the smoker who needs extra motivation to quit smoking. SquareHelp will allow a user to have full-access to the member community of individuals who are on also on the same journey. SquareHelp can meet the smoker where they are at in their journey to quitting and remind them they are not in this alone.

How can SquareHelp help me?

When a user signs up for SquareHelp, they will be required to enter their weekly smoking habits. This information is used to remind them how much money they saved on cigarettes. As a user of SquareHelp they will be able to make verification requests to other users to verify that they are still nicotine free. After a number of days of positive verifications of request, the user will collect points and these points will be used to redeem for gift cards and other perks at retailers.
